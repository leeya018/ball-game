in this game the winner is the one who gain 10 or more points in 3 minutes

you gain points by dragging balls to the target

how is the score rules:

beach ball is 2 points

basket ball is 1 point

each basket ball have a timer of 4 seconds to be frozen on the screen
and then 4 seconds to be drag to the target ,
if you put the ball to target in less then 4 seconds , you gain 1 point ,
but if not , you are loosing 1 point

if you are dragging basketball to the target while there is a beach ball on the screen , you will lose 4 points

each 8 seconds that you will not drag any ball to the target , you will lose 1 points

PAY ATTENTION: you can get a negative score
